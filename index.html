We care and look after children with disabilities who are neglected by the parents and the community. TOURS TO PLACES AND PROVIDE ESSENTIALS NEEDED TO SURVIVE

We take resources and essential items to place where children can't get things such as SOAP, SUGAR, CLOTHINGS, FOOD LIKE (RICE, SPAGHETTI) and other things like BOOKS to read "BIBLE for Christians and QURAN for Muslims", SOLAR lamps for torches because these children and there care givers only survive with traditional lamps which use fuel sometimes it can be really dangerous because there are in reach of children but nothing to do because it is the only affordable available option, so we have provided solar panels with batteries which store power to help and compensate in the night
